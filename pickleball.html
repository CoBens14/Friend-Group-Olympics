Recap

The headline of the event was a blowing wind that kept players on edge, and a marching band competition that kept the games outside. However, the games went on with a live band performance and a passing train. Grant had an outstanding day, dominating on his way to a gold medal and the overall individual points lead. Alexa had another strong performance, finishing with silver and propeling herself into third place overall. Colton rounded out the medal placement with bronze. It was a rough day for Team Chicago, which saw both team members bow out early.
