Rules

Qualification


  • Anybody who receives approval from committee is qualified to participate
  • Must be at least 18 years old
  • Must be Grinnell College or honorary Grinnell College Student

Scoring

  • First place receives 8 points
  • Second place receives 6 points
  • Third place receives 4 points
  • All other participants receive 2 points
  • All who watch/attend but do not participate receive 1 point
